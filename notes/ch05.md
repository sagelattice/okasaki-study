### Splay heaps

- Splay heaps draw some inspiration from quicksort's partition concept. access operations have an implicit restructuring (instead of explicit balance info) to maintain $O(\log n)$ amortized which also causes $O(n)$ in the worst case
- An emergent property of their behavior is that they structurally adapt their efficiency to the data stored in them
